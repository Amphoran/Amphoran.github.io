Aiming
When aiming, you generally want to aim parallel to the ground, the exception being when they are below your y-level. This will allow you to get the most reach possible, by aiming in a straight line to the closest part of your opponent possible. Aiming even slightly slanted can result in your opponent getting the first hit, and you losing the fight.
W-Tapping
W tapping is a method to reset your sprint. After the first hit you land on an opponent, the amount of knockback they will receive from each hit then on, will decrease. In order to always deal the most knockback and make combos easier, reset your sprint by releasing w, and then pressing it down again.

Increasing Damage
To deal increased damage, after you rod your opponent, time your hit at 0.4 seconds after they take knockback from the rod, and continue clicking until after the 0.5 second mark. This will send two damage packets, and will result in you having twice as much burst damage at the start of the engage.
Momentum
Momentum plays a direct role in determining which player gets the first hit. The faster you are moving, the more likely you will get the first hit (basically feeling like increased reach). Jumping to increase your own movement speed and placing blocks to decrease your opponent's, are essential techniques in pvp.
Gamesense
Our site covers many different gamemodes, such as skywars, bedwars, uhc duels, bridge duels, and wool wars. Learn many important situations, like (in skywars for example,) when to prioritize refill over eliminations, knowing whether you should clean a fight or get mid chests, or when you should skip mid entirely and focus on getting stacked.
